# Senior AI Product Manager, Enterprise GenAI Platform

## Company Context

Northstar Cloud is building an enterprise AI copilot platform used by operations, support, and revenue teams. The platform combines retrieval, orchestration, and workflow automation to help large companies deploy domain-specific copilots with governance, observability, and security controls.

We are hiring a Senior AI Product Manager to lead the product strategy and execution for our GenAI platform layer. This person will own the roadmap for model-powered workflows, admin controls, trust features, and platform adoption across enterprise customers.

## Role Summary

You will define what makes our enterprise AI platform credible, scalable, and useful in production. You will work closely with engineering, design, applied AI, solutions, and go-to-market teams to prioritize roadmap bets, ship platform capabilities, and translate customer pain points into high-confidence product decisions.

This role is ideal for a product manager who is comfortable operating across technical platform work and customer-facing business outcomes, and who can communicate clearly about AI product tradeoffs.

## What You Will Own

- Product strategy for our enterprise GenAI platform, including copilots, orchestration, retrieval quality, and trust features
- Roadmap prioritization across model quality, workflow reliability, admin tooling, and enterprise readiness
- Cross-functional execution with engineering, design, applied AI, data, solutions, and GTM teams
- Product requirements for features such as prompt and workflow management, evaluation loops, observability, admin controls, and usage analytics
- KPI definition across activation, retention, workflow success, deflection, user trust, and business impact
- Customer and market discovery to identify high-value AI workflow use cases and platform gaps
- Executive communication on roadmap tradeoffs, launch readiness, and AI product risks

## Must-Have Requirements

- 5+ years of product management experience, including ownership of technical or platform products
- Experience defining product strategy and shipping cross-functional roadmap initiatives
- Strong analytical and experimentation mindset with comfort using metrics to drive prioritization
- Demonstrated interest or experience in AI, LLM, or automation products
- Ability to work closely with engineering on ambiguous technical problems and turn them into clear product decisions
- Strong written and verbal communication with both technical and executive stakeholders
- Experience translating customer pain points into product requirements and outcome-driven roadmaps

## Preferred Requirements

- Direct experience with LLM products, model evaluation, prompt workflows, or enterprise AI platforms
- Familiarity with enterprise requirements such as governance, privacy, compliance, and admin controls
- Exposure to workflow automation, internal developer platforms, or knowledge systems
- Experience launching products in B2B SaaS environments
- Comfort discussing model quality, retrieval quality, latency, reliability, and safety tradeoffs with engineering teams

## Signals We Will Look For In Interviews

- Clear product judgment around where AI creates value versus noise
- Strong examples of leading cross-functional teams through ambiguous execution
- Evidence of using data and customer insight to prioritize product decisions
- Technical fluency with model-powered systems without needing to be an ML engineer
- Good instincts around enterprise credibility, rollout risk, and governance requirements
- Ability to frame a realistic adoption strategy for enterprise AI products
